About SJSU Career Event

The MeetingPoint SJSU career event is all about socializing and networking. It's a great time to introduce yourself to recruiters
from companies you would like to work for following graduation. It's important that you prepare yourself and be confident to those
you want to make a good first impression on. So, the dress code is business casual. You DO NOT have to make a reservation to attend the event
,but you should RSVP on our homepage, so we can plan accordingly. Making a reservation through our website is highly recommended as you can reserve
a lot to talk to specific recruiters across our list of companies!

What To Expect

The event will be hosted at Tower Lawn, so prepare to be outside for a few hours. The dress code WILL be highly enforced.
Outside snacks and drinks are welcome, but there will be student vendors at the even selling their food and beverages.
Pets are welcome, however please have your pet on an appropaiate leash or travel friendly kennel.

Event Information

Date: 8-1-2023
Time: 10:00am to 5:00 pm
