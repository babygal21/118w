ABOUT MEETINGPOINT

MeetingPoint is a highly anticipated event that is exclusively organized by SJSU students, for SJSU students!

The goal of this event is to provide a platform for upcoming graduates to connect with potential employers and to explore career opportunities in a relaxed and comfortable environment. This event is particularly useful for graduating seniors who are seeking entry-level jobs or internships. However, it is not just limited to graduating seniors as all SJSU students are welcome to attend and network with other students. MeetingPoint brings together a wide range of organizations such as Google, Intel, Microsoft, and Cisco to provide valuable insights into the various career paths that are available. These organizations will also offer internship opportunities to students who are interested in getting some practical experience before graduating. Additionally, MeetingPoint offers an excellent opportunity for students to establish meaningful relationships and connections that could be beneficial in their future endeavors.

This event is not just another career fair, but an immersive experience that aims to help students achieve their career aspirations.
