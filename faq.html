FAQs

What is the purpose of this event?

The purpose for MeetingPoint is to give SJSU Students the opportunity to network with professionals from various companies and help guide them to potential internships or full time jobs in the future.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up for this event by clicking the link on the home page. You will get a confirmation email sent to you once you are registered.

Where will this event be located?

This event will be located at SJSU's Tower Lawn. More information regarding the location can be found in our "About" page.

What is the dress code for this event?

You will be meeting professionals from various companies, it is recommended that you wear business attire.

Who can attend this event?

MeetingPoint is open to all current SJSU students.

Do I have to bring anything with me to this event?

We recommend that you bring copies of your resume.

What companies will be attending this event?

In our navigation bar above, by clicking on "Organizations," you will see a list of all companies that will be attending this event.

Is there a cost to attend this event?

No, this event is free for current SJSU students.

Will there be food served at this event?

Unfortunately, no. The main focus of this event is to network with professionals and connect with them for future opportunities.
